Octopus

Octopuses have three hearts. Two of out of the three hearts are for the two gills, the third for the body.

  • Habitat: waters of tropical, subtropical
  • Average length: 24-36 inches
  • Diet: prawns, fish, other octopus
  • Defense abilties: Able to squirt out ink and able to camouflage
